Last weekend, my family and I went camping in the mountains. We arrived in the afternoon and put up our **tent** (*example*) near a small river. After that, we decided to explore the (1) ______________ nearby. My dad wanted to find some (2) ______________ to make a fire for cooking our dinner.

As the sun went down, it became very dark. I used my (3) ______________ to see the path, but suddenly I heard a strange noise behind a tree. It was a bit (4) ______________, but it was just a small rabbit! We walked over a wooden (5) ______________ and finally got back to our camp. We cooked sausages and talked about the stars. It was a wonderful night!

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A boring day at home

* **C.** A trip to the cinema

### ĐÁP ÁN (DÀNH CHO GIÁO VIÊN/PHỤ HUYNH)

**BỘ ĐỀ 1:**

1. **forest** (đi khám phá khu rừng)
2. **wood** (tìm củi để nhóm lửa)
3. **torch** (dùng đèn pin để nhìn đường)
4. **scary** (hơi đáng sợ nhưng chỉ là con thỏ)
5. **bridge** (đi qua cây cầu gỗ)
6. **B. An exciting night in the mountains** (Tiêu đề bao quát toàn bộ chuyến cắm trại)
