CHỦ ĐỀ "A DAY AT THE TV STUDIO" (TRUYỀN HÌNH & TRUYỀN THÔNG)

**Nội dung:** Cuộc trò chuyện về trải nghiệm tham quan đài truyền hình.

**Example:**
**Michael:** It was so exciting to see how they film the news, wasn't it?
**Holly:** **D**

**Questions:**

1. **Michael:** How many people were working in the studio while they were filming?
**Holly:** __________
2. **Michael:** That famous presenter looked much taller on TV than in real life!
**Holly:** __________
3. **Michael:** I'd love to work as a camera operator when I'm older.
**Holly:** __________
4. **Michael:** Do you think we’ll be allowed to see the makeup room later?
**Holly:** __________
5. **Michael:** My dad said that he used to know the director of this show.
**Holly:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** I’m not sure, let’s go and ask the guide.
* **B.** I agree, I was quite surprised when he walked past us!
* **C.** What a small world! Maybe they still talk to each other.
* **D.** Absolutely! I've always wondered what happens behind the scenes. **(Example)**
* **E.** You'd need to be very strong to carry that heavy equipment all day.
* **F.** I couldn't count them all because it was so crowded.
* **G.** He’s been working there for over twenty years.
* **H.** No, I’ve never watched that channel before.
