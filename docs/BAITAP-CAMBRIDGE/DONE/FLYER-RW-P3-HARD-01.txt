

### BỘ ĐỀ 11: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE SECRET OF THE ANCIENT CAVE"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **explorer** (*example*) | **ancient** | **frightened** |
| **carefully** | **whispering** | **discovered** |
| **disappointed** | **flashlight** | **creatures** |
| **strange** |  |  |

My uncle Richard is a famous **explorer** (*example*) who travels to the most remote places on Earth. Last summer, he invited me to join him on a trip to a mountain range. One afternoon, while we were hiking, we (1) ______________ a small opening behind some thick bushes.

It was a cave! We turned on our (2) ______________ and stepped inside. The air was cold, and I felt a bit (3) ______________, but Uncle Richard told me to be brave. On the walls, we saw (4) ______________ paintings of animals that lived thousands of years ago. We moved (5) ______________ so we wouldn't damage anything.

We didn't find any gold or jewels, but we weren't (6) ______________. Finding a piece of history was the best prize ever!

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A scary night in the forest
* **B.** A historical discovery
* **C.** My uncle's new flashlight

---
### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 11:**

1. **discovered** (Khám phá ra một lối vào hang động)
2. **flashlight** (Bật đèn pin để vào hang)
3. **frightened** (Cảm thấy hơi sợ hãi)
4. **ancient** (Những bức tranh cổ đại trên tường)
5. **carefully** (Di chuyển cẩn thận để không làm hỏng vật gì)
6. **B. A historical discovery**
