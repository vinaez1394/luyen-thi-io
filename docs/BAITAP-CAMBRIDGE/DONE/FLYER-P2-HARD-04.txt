 CHỦ ĐỀ "THE VOLCANO PROJECT" (ĐỊA LÝ & KHOA HỌC)

**Nội dung:** Hai học sinh thảo luận về bài thuyết trình nhóm về núi lửa.

**Example:**
**David:** Have you found enough information about the volcano in Iceland?
**Emma:** **F**

**Questions:**

1. **David:** Is it true that the lava can reach temperatures of over 1,000 degrees?
**Emma:** __________
2. **David:** We should probably add some videos to our presentation to make it better.
**Emma:** __________
3. **David:** What would happen if the volcano erupted while people were visiting it?
**Emma:** __________
4. **David:** I’ve forgotten to bring the glue for our model, I’m so sorry!
**Emma:** __________
5. **David:** Shall we finish the rest of the project at my house after school?
**Emma:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** That’s a brilliant idea, it would be much more interesting.
* **B.** Don't worry, I think I've got some in my rucksack.
* **C.** It’s much larger than the one we saw last year.
* **D.** It depends on how close they were to the top.
* **E.** My mother's driving us there at 4 o'clock.
* **F.** Not yet, I’ve been busy studying for the maths test all morning. **(Example)**
* **G.** I’ll check the textbook, but I believe that’s correct.
* **H.** I’d prefer to stay at the library where it’s quieter.
