
### BỘ ĐỀ 6: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE BIG FOOTBALL MATCH"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **stadium** (*example*) | **gold** | **scored** |
| **cup** | **quickly** | **players** |
| **silver** | **expensive** | **match** |
| **believe** |  |  |

Yesterday was a very special day for our school team. We went to the city (1) **stadium** (*example*) to play in the final football competition. There were hundreds of people there watching us.

The (1) ______________ on the other team were very tall and strong, but we weren't afraid. We played the (2) ______________ for over an hour. It was very difficult. Then, in the last five minutes, my friend Harry ran (3) ______________ with the ball and (4) ______________ a great goal!

We were so happy when the whistle blew. The teacher gave us a beautiful (5) ______________ to take back to our school. We all felt like famous stars! My parents took the whole team out for pizza to celebrate our victory.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** Winning the competition
* **B.** Buying new football boots
* **C.** A rainy day in the city

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)



**BỘ ĐỀ 6:**

1. **players** (Các cầu thủ đội bạn rất cao)
2. **match** (Chơi trận đấu trong hơn một giờ)
3. **quickly** (Chạy nhanh với quả bóng)
4. **scored** (Ghi một bàn thắng tuyệt vời)
5. **cup** (Nhận một chiếc cúp đẹp mang về trường)
6. **A. Winning the competition**
