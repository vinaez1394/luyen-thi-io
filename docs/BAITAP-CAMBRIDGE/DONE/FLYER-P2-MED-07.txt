
CHỦ ĐỀ "SHOPPING FOR NEW CLOTHES" (MUA SẮM QUẦN ÁO)

**Nội dung:** Cuộc hội thoại giữa khách hàng và nhân viên bán hàng trong cửa hàng quần áo.

**Example:**
**Assistant:** Can I help you find something today?
**Customer:** **F**

**Questions:**

1. **Assistant:** What size do you usually wear?
**Customer:** __________
2. **Assistant:** Would you like to try on this green jacket?
**Customer:** __________
3. **Assistant:** The changing rooms are over there, next to the mirrors.
**Customer:** __________
4. **Assistant:** Does it fit you well, or is it too big?
**Customer:** __________
5. **Assistant:** How would you like to pay for it?
**Customer:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** By credit card, if that’s okay.
* **B.** It’s a bit too tight around the shoulders.
* **C.** I’ll go and check in the back for you.
* **D.** Medium is usually best for me.
* **E.** No, I think it’s too expensive.
* **F.** Yes, please. I’m looking for a warm coat. **(Example)**
* **G.** Great, I'll be back in a minute.
* **H.** Yes, I’d love to. It looks very nice.
