

### BỘ ĐỀ 5: CHỦ ĐỀ "A VISIT TO THE HOSPITAL"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **hospital** (*example*) | **medicine** | **carefully** |
| **temperature** | **nurse** | **whispered** |
| **broken** | **excited** | **felt** |
| **stomach** |  |  |

Last week, my friend Sarah didn't come to school. I was worried, so my mum took me to see her at the **hospital** (*example*). When we arrived, Sarah was lying in bed. She (1) ______________ very tired and her face was quite red.

A kind (2) ______________ came in to check on her. She used a small machine to take Sarah's (3) ______________. It was very high, so Sarah had to stay in bed and rest. The woman gave her some (4) ______________ to drink. It tasted like strawberries, so Sarah didn't mind taking it.

The room was very quiet, so we (5) ______________ when we talked. Sarah told me she hopes to be better by Saturday because she wants to go to the park. I brought her a funny book to read while she waits to go home.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A noisy day at school
* **B.** Visiting a sick friend
* **C.** A trip to the toy shop

---
### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 5:**

1. **felt** (Cô ấy cảm thấy rất mệt)
2. **nurse** (Một y tá tốt bụng vào kiểm tra)
3. **temperature** (Đo nhiệt độ/sốt)
4. **medicine** (Cho uống thuốc)
5. **whispered** (Thì thầm vì phòng cần yên tĩnh)
6. **B. Visiting a sick friend**
