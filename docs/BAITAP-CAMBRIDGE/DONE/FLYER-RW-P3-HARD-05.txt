David has always been fascinated by space. Every night, he sits on his balcony and looks at the stars through his (example) **telescope**. He dreams of becoming a professional (1) ______________ one day to discover new worlds.

Last Friday, while David was watching the sky, he noticed something (2) ______________. A bright light was flashing in a pattern he had never seen before. He ran to his computer and connected his (3) ______________ to record the data. Suddenly, a strange (4) ______________ appeared on his monitor. It looked like a map of a solar system with three unknown (5) ______________.

David couldn't (6) ______________ his eyes! Was someone from a great (7) ______________ trying to talk to Earth? He decided to send his findings to the national space center. He is still waiting for their call, but he knows that the universe is full of secrets waiting to be found.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**
