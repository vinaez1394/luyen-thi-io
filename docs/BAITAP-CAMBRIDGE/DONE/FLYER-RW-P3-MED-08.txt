
### BỘ ĐỀ 8: CHỦ ĐỀ "A TRIP TO THE DESERT"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

Last winter, my family went to Egypt. The weather was very hot and (1) ______________. I had my first ride on a (example) **camel** named Leo. It felt very (1) ______________ at first because I was so high up, but Leo was very kind.

We traveled across the deep yellow (2) ______________ for two hours. Suddenly, the wind started to blow very hard. Our guide said a sand (3) ______________ was coming, so we had to move (4) ______________ to find a safe place. We hid behind a large rock until the wind stopped.

It was a long (5) ______________, but finally, we saw a giant (6) ______________ in the distance. It was amazing to think that people built it thousands of years ago. I took many photos to show my friends at school.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** An adventure in the sun
* **B.** Learning to swim in Egypt
* **C.** A cold day in the mountains

---

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)



**BỘ ĐỀ 8:**

1. **dry** (Thời tiết nóng và khô)
2. **scary** (Cảm thấy hơi sợ vì ngồi trên cao)
3. **sand** (Đi băng qua cát vàng)
4. **storm** (Cơn bão cát đang đến)
5. **quickly** (Di chuyển nhanh để tìm chỗ trú)
6. **journey** (Một chuyến hành trình dài)
7. **A. An adventure in the sun**
