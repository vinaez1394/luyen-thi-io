

### BỘ ĐỀ 7: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE COOKING COMPETITION"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **kitchen** (*example*) | **together** | **dangerously** |
| **wait** | **smelt** | **flour** |
| **silver** | **strange** | **hungry** |
| **tomorrow** |  |  |

Last Saturday, my sister and I decided to enter a cooking competition. We spent all morning in the **kitchen** (*example*) preparing a special chocolate cake. First, we mixed the sugar, eggs, and (1) ______________ in a large bowl. It was messy, but we had a lot of fun working (2) ______________.

When we put the cake in the oven, the house soon (3) ______________ delicious. I was very (4) ______________ and wanted to eat it immediately, but my sister said we had to (5) ______________ for it to cool down.

When the judges tasted our cake, they smiled and gave us a small (6) ______________ cup. We were so proud! We decided to bake another cake for our grandmother's birthday next week.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A day at the restaurant
* **B.** Baking a winning cake
* **C.** Buying eggs at the shop

---
### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 7:**

1. **flour** (Trộn đường, trứng và bột mì)
2. **together** (Làm việc cùng nhau)
3. **smelt** (Căn nhà có mùi thơm - Quá khứ của *smell*)
4. **hungry** (Cảm thấy đói và muốn ăn ngay)
5. **wait** (Phải chờ bánh nguội)
6. **B. Baking a winning cake**
