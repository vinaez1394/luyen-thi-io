
### BỘ ĐỀ 4: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE SPACE MUSEUM"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

Yesterday, my class went on a trip to the **museum** (*example*) in the city centre. We went there to learn about space for our (1) ______________ project. When we arrived, we saw a huge silver (2) ______________. It was so tall that it nearly touched the ceiling!

A man who was a real (3) ______________ talked to us about his job. He showed us a suit that people wear in space. He said it is very (4) ______________ and difficult to walk in. We also went into a dark room to see a show about the (5) ______________ like Mars and Jupiter.

I bought a small toy robot in the shop before we went home. I want to be a pilot when I grow up so I can fly to the moon!

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** Learning about the stars
* **B.** A visit to the toy shop
* **C.** My favorite teacher

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)


**BỘ ĐỀ 4:**

1. **science** (dự án môn khoa học)
2. **rocket** (tên lửa bạc khổng lồ)
3. **astronaut** (phi hành gia thật kể về công việc)
4. **heavy** (bộ đồ phi hành gia rất nặng)
5. **planets** (các hành tinh như Sao Hỏa, Sao Mộc)
6. **A. Learning about the stars**
