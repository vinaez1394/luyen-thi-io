
CHỦ ĐỀ "THE NEW MUSIC CLUB" (CLB ÂM NHẠC)

**Nội dung:** Hai người bạn nói về việc tham gia một câu lạc bộ ngoại khóa ở trường.

**Example:**
**George:** How long have you been in the music club, Betty?
**Betty:** **D**

**Questions:**

1. **George:** Which instrument would you like to learn to play?
**Betty:** __________
2. **George:** How often do you have to practice?
**Betty:** __________
3. **George:** Is it difficult to read the music?
**Betty:** __________
4. **George:** Who is the teacher this year?
**Betty:** __________
5. **George:** Can I come and watch your concert next week?
**Betty:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** It’s Mrs. Green, she’s very kind.
* **B.** I’ve played the piano since I was six.
* **C.** Every Tuesday and Thursday after school.
* **D.** I joined it about three years ago. **(Example)**
* **E.** Only if you don't make any noise!
* **F.** Actually, I prefer the violin to the guitar.
* **G.** It's at the end of the long hallway.
* **H.** It was at first, but it’s easier now.

---

### ĐÁP ÁN VÀ PHÂN TÍCH KỸ THUẬT

F	Lựa chọn giữa các loại nhạc cụ (Which instrument).
C	Trả lời về tần suất (How often).
H	Trả lời về độ khó (Is it difficult - Quá khứ vs Hiện tại).
A	Trả lời về danh tính người (Who).
E	Lời đồng ý kèm theo một điều kiện (Only if).
