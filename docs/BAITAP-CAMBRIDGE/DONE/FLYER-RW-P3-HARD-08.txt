
### BỘ ĐỀ 18: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE ROBOT COMPETITION"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **competition** (*example*) | **clever** | **metal** |
| **quickly** | **difficult** | **screen** |
| **information** | **believe** | **suddenly** |
| **programmed** |  |  |

My school held a big (example) **competition** for young inventors last month. My friend Bobby and I decided to build a robot that could help carry heavy books. We made the body of the robot out of light (1) ______________ so it could move easily.

Bobby is very (2) ______________ with computers, so he (3) ______________ the robot to follow us when we called its name. It wasn't easy; in fact, it was quite (4) ______________ to get the legs to move at the same time. We spent many hours looking at the computer (5) ______________ to fix the problems.

On the day of the contest, our robot ran (6) ______________ across the room and picked up a large box of books. The judges were very impressed! We won first prize, and I still can't (7) ______________ that we built something so amazing ourselves.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A new computer for the classroom
* **B.** Our winning invention
* **C.** Learning to fix a broken car

---

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 18:**

1. **metal** (Làm thân robot bằng kim loại nhẹ)
2. **clever** (Bobby rất thông minh/giỏi máy tính)
3. **programmed** (Cậu ấy đã lập trình cho robot)
4. **difficult** (Việc điều khiển chân robot khá khó khăn)
5. **screen** (Nhìn vào màn hình máy tính để sửa lỗi)
6. **quickly** (Robot chạy nhanh qua căn phòng)
7. **believe** (Vẫn không thể tin được là đã thắng)
8. **B. Our winning invention**
