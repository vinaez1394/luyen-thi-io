Our school has a club for students who care about the **environment** (*example*). This month, we went to the Green Valley to study the local (1) ______________. Our teacher told us that some birds in this area are now (2) ______________ because the river has become too (3) ______________ for them to live near.

We sat quietly in the tall grass and used our (4) ______________ to look for the birds. Suddenly, my friend (5) ______________ a nest high up in an old oak tree. We wrote down a lot of (6) ______________ about what we saw to help the local (7) ______________ with their research.

It was a (8) ______________ day of work because it was very hot, but we felt happy. We are going to start a project to clean the river next week so the birds can return safely.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A lesson about rare birds
