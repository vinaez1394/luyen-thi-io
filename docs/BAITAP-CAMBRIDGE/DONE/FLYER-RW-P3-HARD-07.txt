

### BỘ ĐỀ 17: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE ENDANGERED FOREST"

Last summer, my aunt, who is a famous **forest** (*example*), took me to a remote part of the jungle. She is a (1) ______________ who studies rare birds. She told me that many species have (2) ______________ because the (3) ______________ of the Earth is getting warmer.

We had to walk very (4) ______________ through the thick trees so we wouldn't frighten the animals. I used my (5) ______________ to look at the top of the trees. Suddenly, I saw a bird with bright blue and silver feathers. It was beautiful! My aunt was very excited and wrote down a lot of (6) ______________ in her notebook. It was a long day, but I learned how important it is to protect our nature.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A dangerous walk in the city
* **B.** Searching for rare birds
* **C.** How to climb a tall tree

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 17:**

1. **scientist** (Dì tôi là một nhà khoa học nghiên cứu chim hiếm)
2. **disappeared** (Nhiều loài đã biến mất)
3. **temperature** (Nhiệt độ trái đất nóng lên)
4. **slowly** (Đi bộ thật chậm qua lùm cây)
5. **binoculars** (Dùng ống nhòm để nhìn ngọn cây)
6. **information** (Ghi chép thông tin vào sổ)
7. **B. Searching for rare birds**
