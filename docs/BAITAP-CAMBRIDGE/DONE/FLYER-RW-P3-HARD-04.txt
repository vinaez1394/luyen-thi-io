Yesterday, our science class climbed to the top of a high hill to visit the weather **station** (*example*). We wanted to learn how people know if it will be sunny or rainy. A woman named Mary showed us many different (1) ______________ that measure the wind and the rain.

She explained that they check the (2) ______________ every hour to see how hot or cold it is. We watched her write down all the (3) ______________ on a large map. "The (4) ______________ is very important to everyone," she said. "Farmers need to know about the rain, and pilots need to know about the wind."

While we were there, the (5) ______________ turned grey and we saw many dark clouds. Mary told us that a (6) ______________ was coming, so we had to leave (7) ______________ before the rain started. It was a fascinating trip, and I learned that science is everywhere!
