### BỘ ĐỀ 20: CHỦ ĐỀ "AT THE LIBRARY" (TẠI THƯ VIỆN)

**Nội dung:** Cuộc hội thoại giữa thủ thư và một học sinh đang tìm sách.

**Example:**
**Librarian:** Hello there. Can I help you find a particular book?
**Student:** **E**

**Questions:**

1. **Librarian:** What kind of stories do you prefer reading?
**Student:** __________
2. **Librarian:** How many books are you allowed to take home at once?
**Student:** __________
3. **Librarian:** When do I have to bring them back to the library?
**Student:** __________
4. **Librarian:** Is there a quiet place where I can study for my exams?
**Student:** __________
5. **Librarian:** Would you like me to show you where the computers are?
**Student:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** I’ve already read that one twice.
* **B.** Yes, there are some desks on the second floor.
* **C.** Up to four at a time with your student card.
* **D.** That would be very useful, thank you.
* **E.** Yes, please. I’m looking for something about space. **(Example)**
* **F.** I’m mostly interested in science and history.
* **G.** You need to return them within two weeks.
* **H.** It’s open from nine until five every day.
