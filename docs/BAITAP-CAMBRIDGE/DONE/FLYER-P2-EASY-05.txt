
### BỘ ĐỀ 5: CHỦ ĐỀ "A NEW HOBBY" (SỞ THÍCH & TRÒ CHƠI)

**Nội dung:** Hai người bạn nói về một trò chơi mới mua.

**Example:**
**Jane:** Look at this new board game I bought!
**Jack:** **E**

**Questions:**

1. **Jane:** Is it a computer game?
**Jack:** __________
2. **Jane:** How many people can play it?
**Jack:** __________
3. **Jane:** Is it difficult to learn?
**Jack:** __________
4. **Jane:** Where shall we play it?
**Jack:** __________
5. **Jane:** Do you want to start now?
**Jack:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** Between two and four players.
* **B.** In the garden, at the wooden table.
* **C.** I bought it at the shop near here.
* **D.** No, it's quite simple, actually.
* **E.** Wow! It looks really interesting. **(Example)**
* **F.** Yes, let’s go!
* **G.** No, it’s a card game about space.
* **H.** I played football yesterday.

---

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

G (Computer game -> Card game)
A (How many people -> 2-4 players)
D (Difficult -> Simple)
B (Where play -> Garden/Table)
F (Start now -> Let's go)
