My father is a computer hardware **specialist** (*example*) who builds very powerful machines. Last week, he invited me to his office to show me a new (1) ______________ that he had just finished. It was designed to help children learn about web logic and AI.

I sat down and began to type my name on the (2) ______________. At first, nothing happened, but my father told me to wait (3) ______________. (4) ______________, the screen filled with bright lights and a friendly robot appeared! The robot asked me to solve a difficult math problem.

When I finished, the robot gave me some (5) ______________ about the history of computers. It was a wonderful (6) ______________ for me. I didn't want to go home because I felt like a real IT expert for the day!

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** Buying a new toy robot
* **B.** A day at the technology office
* **C.** How to fix a broken laptop
