

### BỘ ĐỀ 9: CHỦ ĐỀ "THE NEW INVENTION"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

Last month, my older brother had to finish a special **project** (*example*) for his school. He wanted to build a small robot that could tidy up his room. He spent many days working in the garage. First, he made the body out of light (1) ______________ so it wasn't too heavy to move.

He asked me to help him put the (2) ______________ inside so the robot would have power. We followed the instructions very (3) ______________ because we didn't want to make a mistake. When we finished, the (4) ______________ looked like a small silver dog.

My brother (5) ______________, "Let's turn it on!" The robot started to move and began picking up all the socks on the floor. It was amazing! My brother got an 'A' for his work, and now his room is always clean.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A robot that loves to cook
* **B.** The clever school invention
* **C.** Buying a new toy dog

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 9:**

1. **metal** (Thân robot làm bằng kim loại nhẹ)
2. **batteries** (Lắp pin để có năng lượng)
3. **carefully** (Theo dõi hướng dẫn cẩn thận)
4. **machine** (Cỗ máy trông giống một chú chó bạc nhỏ)
5. **whispered** (Thì thầm vì hồi hộp/phấn khích)
6. **B. The clever school invention**
