

### BỘ ĐỀ 3: CHỦ ĐỀ "A DAY AT THE SEA"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **beach** (*example*) | **expensive** | **carefully** |
| **island** | **dolphins** | **whispered** |
| **shells** | **waves** | **forgot** |
| **dangerous** |  |  |

Last Tuesday, it was very hot, so my aunt took me to the **beach** (*example*) for the day. We carried our bags across the sand and found a good place to sit. The water was beautiful and blue. While I was swimming, I saw two (1) ______________ jumping in the water. They were very friendly!

After my swim, I walked along the shore to look for some (2) ______________. I found five very large ones to take home. Then, we decided to take a small boat to a little (3) ______________ that wasn't far away. The (4) ______________ were a bit high, so the boat moved up and down a lot. It was very exciting!

I wanted to take a photo, but I (5) ______________ my camera in the car! Aunt Mary said, "Don't worry, we can come back tomorrow." It was the best day of my summer holiday.

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A long walk in the park
* **B.** A fun day by the water
* **C.** A new camera for my birthday

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

**BỘ ĐỀ 3:**

1. **dolphins** (nhìn thấy cá heo nhảy dưới nước)
2. **shells** (tìm vỏ sò dọc bờ biển)
3. **island** (đi thuyền ra một hòn đảo nhỏ)
4. **waves** (sóng hơi cao làm thuyền dập dìu)
5. **forgot** (quên máy ảnh trong xe)
6. **B. A fun day by the water**
