
### BỘ ĐỀ 22: CHỦ ĐỀ "A DAY IN THE PARK"

**READ THE STORY. CHOOSE A WORD FROM THE BOX. WRITE THE CORRECT WORD NEXT TO NUMBERS 1–5. THERE IS ONE EXAMPLE.**

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **park** (*example*) | **sun** | **ball** |
| **ran** | **ducks** | **grass** |
| **forest** | **snow** | **scary** |

Today the weather was beautiful, so I went to the **park** (*example*) with my dog, Lucky. The (1) ______________ was very bright and the sky was blue. I took a red (2) ______________ out of my bag and threw it. Lucky (3) ______________ very fast to catch it and brought it back to me.

We saw some (4) ______________ swimming in the small lake. They were very cute. Later, I felt tired, so I sat on the green (5) ______________ and ate my sandwich. Lucky sat next to me and watched the birds. We stayed there all afternoon because it was so much fun!

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** A fun day with my dog
* **B.** A walk in the dark forest
* **C.** Finding a lost ball

---

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)



**BỘ ĐỀ 22:**

1. **sun** (mặt trời)
2. **ball** (quả bóng)
3. **ran** (đã chạy)
4. **ducks** (những con vịt)
5. **grass** (bãi cỏ)
6. **A. A fun day with my dog**
