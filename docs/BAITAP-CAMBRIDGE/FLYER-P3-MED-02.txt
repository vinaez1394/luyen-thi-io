My uncle Harry lives on a farm. Last summer, I stayed there for a week. Every morning, we woke up (1) ______________ at 5 o’clock. My first job was to (2) ______________ the chickens and the cows. They were always very hungry!

In the afternoon, Uncle Harry let me sit on his big green (3) ______________. We went to the large garden to pick some (4) ______________ like carrots and potatoes for our lunch. Everything was very fresh. Suddenly, the sky turned grey and there were many dark (5) ______________. We ran inside just before the rain started. I learned that life on a farm is hard work but very fun!

**(6) Now choose the best name for the story. Write a letter:**

* **A.** Helping on the farm
