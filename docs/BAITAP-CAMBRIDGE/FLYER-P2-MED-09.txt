

### BỘ ĐỀ 18: CHỦ ĐỀ "A NEW PET" (THÚ CƯNG MỚI)

**Nội dung:** Hai bạn nhỏ nói chuyện về chú chó mới mà một bạn vừa được tặng.

**Example:**
**Tom:** Did you finally get that dog you wanted for your birthday?
**Mary:** **F**

**Questions:**

1. **Tom:** He looks very energetic! What is he like?
**Mary:** __________
2. **Tom:** Who usually looks after him during the day?
**Mary:** __________
3. **Tom:** Where does he sleep at night?
**Mary:** __________
4. **Tom:** Have you taught him any clever tricks yet?
**Mary:** __________
5. **Tom:** I’d love to take him for a walk in the park with you.
**Mary:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** In a soft basket in the corner of the kitchen.
* **B.** Usually my older brother, but I help too.
* **C.** He’s been eating a lot of biscuits lately.
* **D.** That sounds fun! Shall we go this afternoon?
* **E.** No, he’s much bigger than a cat.
* **F.** Yes! My parents surprised me with him yesterday. **(Example)**
* **G.** Yes, he can already sit and stay when I ask.
* **H.** He’s quite small, but he’s very friendly.
