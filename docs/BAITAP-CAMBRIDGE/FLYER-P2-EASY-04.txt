
### BỘ ĐỀ 4: CHỦ ĐỀ "HELPING DAD IN THE KITCHEN" (BẾP NÚC & ĐỒ ĂN)

**Nội dung:** Con giúp bố chuẩn bị bữa tối.

**Example:**
**Dad:** Can you help me cook dinner, Mary?
**Mary:** **F**

**Questions:**

1. **Dad:** Great! What are we making tonight?
**Mary:** __________
2. **Dad:** Where are the tomatoes? I can't see them.
**Mary:** __________
3. **Dad:** Can you wash them for me, please?
**Mary:** __________
4. **Dad:** Do you like olives on your pizza?
**Mary:** __________
5. **Dad:** Is the oven hot yet?
**Mary:** __________

**Options (A-H):**

* **A.** No, I hate them!
* **B.** They are in the small brown bag.
* **C.** Yes, it’s 200 degrees now.
* **D.** I'm wearing my favorite apron.
* **E.** Of course, I'll do it now.
* **F.** Yes, I’d love to, Dad! **(Example)**
* **G.** A big cheese and tomato pizza.
* **H.** I ate a sandwich for lunch.

---

### ĐÁP ÁN (ANSWER KEY)

G (What making -> Pizza)
B (Where/Tomatoes -> In the bag)
E (Wash them -> I'll do it)
A (Like olives -> Hate them)
C (Oven hot -> 200 degrees)
